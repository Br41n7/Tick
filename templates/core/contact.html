Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?
Simply browse our events, select the one you're interested in, choose your tickets, and complete the secure payment. You'll receive a digital ticket via email and SMS.
Can I get a refund for my booking?
Refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event, subject to the event organizer's cancellation policy. Check your booking details for specific terms.
How do I become an event host?
Sign up for an account, complete your profile, and request to become a host. Our team will review your application and provide feedback within 5 business days.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and work with trusted payment processors. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.
How do I follow an artist?
Visit any artist's profile and click the "Follow" button. You'll receive updates about their new reels, upcoming events, and announcements.
Can I upload reels as an artist?
Yes! Once you upgrade to an artist account, you can upload reels directly from your dashboard. Share your content and engage with your fans.
